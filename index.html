God of Darkness


God of Darkness is a single-player adventure game in which the god of darkness,
the Nameless One, raids the capital ... of the country ... in which you live.
He wants to turn the world into darkness. But the gods of light have
chosen you to defeat the Nameless One...

Let your adventure begin...
